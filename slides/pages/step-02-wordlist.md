# Step 2 – Get a Good Crossword Dictionary

- Crosswords use their own dictionaries, called "word lists".
- Word lists are typically "scored", i.e. more desirable words are given higher priority when finding a fillable grid.
- Higher-quality word lists produce more entertaining puzzles, so getting a good one helps your odds of getting an accepted submission.
- These word lists function as a **starting point**. You _need_ to curate word scores instead of assuming the starting point is good enough.

<br/>

> Note: I score my wordlist using a tagging system. This way, I can rescore all words under a certain tag at once. This is helpful as different types of crosswords prioritize different types of words.

# Step 7 – Share Your Crossword!

<div>

Not only do you get to enjoy the fruits of your labor, but you also get to see how your puzzle works in practice. Playtesting ensures the theme of your crossword is recognizable and whether players found any particular parts of the puzzle too straightforward or too tricky.

I like to open a group session in Crossworthy to look at what how players navigate the puzzle and which words they attempt to fill in.

<br/>

> If you can get feedback earlier in the crossword construction process, that's helpful too!

# Step 2 – Crossword Dictionaries

<div>

High-quality word lists:

- [The Collaborative Word List](https://github.com/Crossword-Nexus/collaborative-word-list)
- [Spread the Wordlist](https://www.spreadthewordlist.com/)
- ⭐ [XWord Info Word Lists](https://www.xwordinfo.com/WordList) 💲

Supplemental word lists:

- [John Kugelman's Wordlist](https://github.com/jkugelman/crossword/blob/main/wordlists/jkugelman-wordlist.txt)
  - Modernizes the sensibilities used by the XWord Info Word List.

Other options:

- [Matt Abate's Wordlist](https://github.com/mattabate/wordlist)
- [Peter Broda's Wordlist](http://www.peterbroda.me/crosswords/wordlist/)

Also:

- [Google Ngram Viewer](https://books.google.com/ngrams/)
  - Look up words by popularity.
- ⭐ [Onelook](https://onelook.com/):
  - Search across dictionaries.
- [Wikipedia Regex Search](https://crosswordnexus.com/wiki/)
  - Find terms not in dictionaries.
- ⭐ [Wordlisted](https://aaronson.org/wordlisted/)
  - Search through your wordlists.

</div>

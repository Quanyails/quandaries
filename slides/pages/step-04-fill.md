# Step 4 – Fill Your Grid

<div>

1. Place your theme entries in the grid.
2. Add black squares (within reason) until your grid shows up as fillable.
3. Find "good entries" for each slot.

If you get stuck, try a different arrangement! Failing that, see if lowering the minimum word score helps.

<br/>

> Note: If you find yourself struggling to make a satisfying grid, consider checking out some of the resources for crossword resources linked to earlier.  There is a book's worth of undescribed theory I'm glossing over here.

</div>

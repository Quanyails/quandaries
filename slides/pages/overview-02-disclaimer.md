# Is this presentation for you?

<div>

You may be interested in this presentation if:

- You are already familiar with the concept of "American-style" crosswords.
- You want to create a crossword using the standards prescribed by major crossword publishers.
- You are looking for a quick how-to rather than theory.

<br/>

> Disclaimer: I have two crosswords published by the Los Angeles Times. I am not qualified to give advice on how to construct crosswords that may be accepted by other publications, e.g. the New York Times.

</div>

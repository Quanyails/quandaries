# Step 0 – Learn

<div>To construct crosswords, you need to know what goes into making a good one. To do this, choose all of the following:</div>

1. Play a range of crosswords. Some crosswords will "feel good", and some will not. You will pick up the rules of crosswords this way.
    - ⭐ [Daily Crossword Links](https://dailycrosswordlinks.com/)
2. Read players' thoughts:
    - ⭐ [Crossword Fiend](https://crosswordfiend.com/)
    - [XWord Info](https://www.xwordinfo.com/) (💲 after _n_ articles)
    - [Rex Parker Does the NYT Crossword](https://rexwordpuzzle.blogspot.com/) 🔥
4. Read up on resources for crossword constructors:
    - ⭐ [Crossword Publication Specs](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/12HGynb2VpJR2akQPT7iCMUBXxqcwzmk3kJjic8F24Kk/edit)
    - ⭐ [Crossword Constructor's Handbook](https://aframegames.com/store/?download=21) 💲
    - ⭐ [OLLI Course: Constructing Crosswords](https://communicrossings.com/olli-course-constructing-crosswords)
    - ⭐ [Wayward Grids](https://www.crosserville.com/waywardGrids/) (rejected crossword submissions with feedback)

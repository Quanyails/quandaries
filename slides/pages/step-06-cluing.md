# Step 6 – Write Clues

<div>

- Clues should be fun but fair, i.e. the part of speech of the clue should match the part of speech of the answer.
- Use a wide range of "clue types", i.e. don't rely purely on dictionary definitions. E.g. you can also:
  - Clue by example
  - Reference pop culture
  - Make a pun

<br/>

> Note: Cluing determines how difficult your crossword is. You can crank up the difficulty of a crossword by relying on trickier clues.

</div>

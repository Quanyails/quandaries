# Workflow

Crossword construction software tends to use the following workflow:

1. Provide the app with a dictionary to be used.
2. Configure your crossword grid (width, height, symmetry).
1. Input the initial words you want in your puzzle.
1. Configure black squares.
1. Find good words for each slot.
1. Add clues for each word.
1. Share your crossword!
